ABOUT US

FEBRUARY 2022 - Current

Althea is the fashion industry's go-to place. Althea, which was founded in February 2022, curates the greatest range of apparel and accessories under one roof, connecting them to customers all over the world. Althea has a large social media following and has successfully created its brand in the hearts of women.

Althea, with its unique partnerships with designers and celebrities, is widely regarded as a market pioneer with a mission to mainstream trendy fashion. New arrivals occur on a regular basis, keeping the site current and relevant. The Althea Group began as an e-commerce-only company and has now expanded to 5 pop-up locations and counting, with the goal of becoming the top omni channel platform via ongoing technological breakthroughs and upgrades.
